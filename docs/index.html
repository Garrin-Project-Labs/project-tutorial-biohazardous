How to survive

Press Prepare To Die to start, or press R to reset and instantly restart.

🚀 Use arrow keys or WASD to move the rocket.

Dodge the spinning runes: ☄ ᚠ ᚢ ᚦ ᚨ ᚱ ᚲ ᚷ ᛉ ᛟ ✹ ☽

Near misses give +1. Meteor dodges are worth +1 more each level.

Every 3 level-ups rotates the game screen, slows the next meteor wave for a moment, changes the outside tentacles, and warns: “be prepared.”

☢ Relic: +13 score, +5 combo, and reveals fate text. At max combo it gives +313 score multiplied by your combo instead.

👁️ Inverted eye: resets game speed until the next level, without changing your level.

🔮 The Third Eye: appears after level 6, reduces speed by 3 levels but never below speed level 3, and has a 52 second cooldown.

🕳 Black hole: plays a space sound, pulls powerups toward you for 13 seconds, then waits 26 seconds before spawning again.

⛧ Pentagram: appears after rotation, resets the canvas angle, and clears meteors.
