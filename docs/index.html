How to survive

Press Prepare To Die to start, or press R to reset and instantly restart.

🚀 Use arrow keys or WASD to move the rocket. Stay above the mouth.

Dodge the spinning runes and meteors: ☄ ᚠ ᚢ ᚦ ᚨ ᚱ ᚲ ᚷ ᛉ ᛟ ✹ ☽

Near misses give +1. Meteor dodges are worth more as the level rises.

Catch falling TRANSCEND letters with the mouth to fill the word. If the player touches those letters, the mouth rejects them and spits them back out as hazards.

When TRANSCEND completes, the word flies up black, slams into the mouth as rainbow text, and raises your TRANSCENDENCE counter.

Every 3 level-ups rotates the screen, slows the next meteor wave, changes the rune border, and warns: “be prepared.”

TRANSCENDENCE 3: The Game Lies — the HUD and warnings become untrustworthy for a moment.

TRANSCENDENCE 5: Eye Boss — the eye turns purple, blinks, charges white warning lines to orange, then fires purple lasers.

TRANSCENDENCE 7: the evil eye dies and White Void begins. Survive it for 26 seconds while scores are worth x10.

TRANSCENDENCE 9: TRANSCEND letters keep spawning in White Void until you reach 9. Then letters stop; survive the timer to enter New Game+, become the mouth, get x3 score, and add one extra falling TRANSCEND letter per loop.
