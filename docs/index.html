How to survive

Press Prepare To Die to start, or press R to reset and instantly restart.

Use arrow keys or WASD to move and dodge the falling white meteors.

Near misses give +1. Meteor dodges are worth +1 more each level.

Every 3 level-ups rotates the game screen.

🧿 Relic: +13 score and reveals fate text.

👁️ Eye: resets game speed until the next level, without changing your level.

⛧ Pentagram: appears after rotation and resets the canvas angle.
